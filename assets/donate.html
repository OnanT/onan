Frequently Asked Questions

How are donations used?

100% of your donation goes directly to the initiative you select. We maintain complete transparency about fund allocation through quarterly reports published on our blog.

Administrative costs are covered separately, so every dollar you donate supports the actual projects and communities.

Can I get a receipt for tax purposes?

Yes! All donations through PayPal automatically generate a receipt that can be used for tax purposes. For recurring donations, you'll receive an annual summary of your contributions.

For donations over $250, we provide an official donation letter upon request.

Can I donate anonymously?

Absolutely. During the PayPal donation process, you can choose to make your donation anonymous. We respect your privacy and won't share your information without permission.

Even if you donate anonymously, we'll still send you updates about how your contribution is making a difference (unless you opt out).

What other ways can I support besides money?

There are many valuable ways to contribute beyond financial support:

  • Contribute to our open-source projects on GitHub
  • Share our initiatives on social media
  • Volunteer your skills (design, development, documentation)
  • Participate in community events
  • Provide educational resources for Kalinago youth

Contact us to discuss how you can contribute your skills and time.

How can I track the impact of my donation?

We provide regular updates through several channels:

  • Quarterly impact reports on our blog
  • Project-specific updates for GreyWolf and Kalinago initiatives
  • Email newsletters for supporters
  • Transparent financial reports

For specific inquiries about how your donation was used, feel free to contact us directly.
